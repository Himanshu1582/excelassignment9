Bodyweight workouts are exercise routines that use a person's own bodyweight to create resistance and improve strength, flexibility, and endurance, as well as coordination.


  1. Tuck jump

  2. Shoulder bridge

    1. Cross that bridge when you come to it.

    2. Lie faceup with your knees bent and your feet hip-width apart.

    3. Place your arms at your sides and lift your spine and hips. Only your head, feet, arms, and shoulders should be on the floor.





  3. Bicycle

    1. Just keep your helmet in the closet.

    2. Lie faceup with your knees bent and your hands behind your head.

    3. Bring your knees in toward your chest.

    4. Bring your right elbow toward your left knee as your right leg straightens.

    5. Continue alternating sides like you're pedaling a bike.
